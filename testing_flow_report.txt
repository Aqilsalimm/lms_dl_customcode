  `MIDTRANS_CLIENT_KEY` and `MIDTRANS_SERVER_KEY` environment variables in
  `.env` (these keys are sandbox credentials).
- Restarted the container to apply the env change.
- Confirmed the frontend build after recent logo updates:
